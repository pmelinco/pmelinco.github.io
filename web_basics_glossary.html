Important Terminology in the Dune Universe

This page contains definitons and information about some of the terms that appear in Dune, the novel, and that are relevant to the universe. This list is not exhaustive, but covers many of the most important terms from the first book of the series. All definitions are taken from the dune bibliography included with the 2005 edition of the book, copyrighted under Brian Herbert.

Term Pronunciation Category Definition Associated with:
Baliset (baal-ih-set) noun A nine string instrument, lineal of descendant of the zithra, tuned to the Chusuk scale and played by strumming. Gurney Halleck
Battle of Corrin Historical Event The space battle from which the Imperial House Corrino took its name. The battle fought near Sigma Draconis in the year 88 B.G. settled the ascendancy of the ruling House from Salusa Secundus Padishah Emperor; House Corrino
Butlerian Jihad (but-LEHR-ee-an jee-HAD) Historical Event The crusade against computers, thinking machines, and conscious robots began in 201 BG and concluded in 108 BG. Its chief commandment remains in the OC Bible as "Thou shalt not make a mchine in the likeness of the human mind
Caladan (cal-AH-daan) planet third planet of Delta Pavonis; birthworld of Paul Atreides Paul Atreides; House Atreides; Sea
Carryall noun A flying wing, the aerial workhorse of Arrakis used to transport large spice mining, hunting, and refining equipment. Arrakis
Crysknife (cris-nife) noun The sacred knife of the Fremen on Arrakis. It is manufactured in two forms from teeth taken from dead sandworms (see below). The two forms are fixed and unfixed. an unfixed knife requires proximity to a human body’s electrical field to prevent disintegration and fixed knives are treated for storage. all are about 20 cm long Fremen; sandworms
Dictum Familia (dic-TUHM fam-EE-lee-ah) noun That rule of the Great Convention which prohibits the slaying of a royal person or member of a Great House by informal treachery. The rule sets up the formal outline an dlimits the means of assassination. Great Convention; Assassination; feuds
Drum Sand noun Impaction of sand in such a way that any suddenly blow against its surface Fremen; Desert; Sand
Erg (uhr-guh) noun An extensive dune area, a sea of sand Arrakis; Desert; dune
Eyes of Ibad (eyes of ee-BAD) charateristic The characteristic effect of a diet high in melange where the whites and pupils of the eyes turn a deep blue Fremen; spice
Fedaykin (fed-ayy-keen) noun Fremen death commandos; historically: a group formed and pledged to give their lives to right a wrong Fremen; militia
Filt-plug noun A nose filter worn with a stillsuit to capture moisture from exhaled breath Stillsuit
Free Traders noun Idiomatic term for smugglers trade; commerce
Frigate (frih-git) noun the largest spaceships that can be grounded on a planet and take off in one piece travel; trade
Galach (gal-ah-CKH) language The official language of the Imperium. Hybrid Inglo-Slavic with strong traces of cultural-specialisation terms adopted during the long chain of human migrations Imperium
Giedi Prime (gee-eh-dee prime) planet The planet of Ophiuchi B, homeworld of the Harkonnen; a median-viable planet with low-active photosynthesis range House Harkonnen
Gom Jabbar (gohm jah-bar) noun The high-handed enemy; that specific poison needle used by the Bene Gesserit Proctors in the death-alternative test of human awareness Bene Gesserit; fear
Great Convention treaty The universal truce enforced under the power balance maintained by the Guild, the Great Houses, and the Imperium. Its chief rule prohibits the use of atomic weapons against human targets Imperium
Harvester noun A large spice mining machine commonly employed on rich, uncontaminated melange blows Arrakis, spice, trade
Hiereg (uhr-guh) place A temporary Fremen desert camp on open sand Fremen; desert
House noun Idiomatic for Ruling Clan of a planet or planetary system status
Houses Major status Holders of planetary fiefs; interplanetary entrepreneurs power
Houses Minor status Planet-bound entrepreneur class power
Judge of the Change (uhr-guh) noun An official appointed by the Lansraad High Council and the emperor to monitor a change of fief, a kanly negotiable, or formal battle in a war of assassins. the Judge’s arbitral authority may be challenged only before the High Council with the emperor present Lasraad; Liet-Kynes
Kwisatz Haderach (kwee-sat-z had-er-ack) noun (lit. “shortening of the way”) This is the label applied by the Bene Gesserit to the unknown for which they sought a genetic solution: a male Bene Gesserit whose organic mental powers would bridge space and time Bene Gesserit; Paul Atreides
melange (spice) (may-lawn-jeh) drug The crop for which Arrakis is the unique source. The spice is mildly addictive when taken in small quantities, severely addictive when imbibed in quantities abovetwo grams daily per seventy kilos of body weight Fremen; Eyes of Ibad; premonitory skills
Missionaria Protectiva organisation The arm of the Bene Gesserit order charged with sowing infection superstition on primitive worlds, thus opening those regions to exploitation by the Bene Gesserit Bene Gesserit; missionaires
Muad'dib (moo-ahd-dib) animal The adapted kangaroo mouse of Arrakis, a creature associated in the Fremen’s earth spirit mythology with a design visible on the planet’s second moon Arrakis; Paul Atreides
Orange Catholic (OC) Bible (uhr-guh) religious text The “Accumulated Book,” the religious text produced by the Commission of Ecumenical Translators. It contains elements of most ancient religions , including the Maometh Saari, Mahayana Christianity, Zensunni Catholicism, and Buddislamic traditions Religion
Ornithopter (or-nih-thop-terr) noun Any aircraft capable of sustained wing-beat flight in the manner of birds. flight; aircraft
Reverend Mother title Originally, a proctor of the Bene Gesserit, one who has transformed an illuminating poison within her body, raising herself to a higher state of awareness Bene Gesserit; Helen Gaius Mohaim
Salusa Secundus (sah-loo-saa seh-con-dus) planet Third planet of Gamma Waiping; designated Imperial Prison Planet after removal of the Royal Court to Kaitain. Salusa Secundus is the homeworld of House Corrino, and the second stopping point in migration of the Wanderinng Zensunni. House Corrino; Imperium; Sardaukar
Sandwalker (uhr-guh) title Any Fremen trained to survive in the open desert Fremen
Sandworm / Shai-Hulud (shaay-hoo-lood) creature The sandworms of the planet Arrakis, which grow to enormous size and live to great age unless slain by one of their fellows or drowned in water, which is poisonous to them Arrakis; Fremen
Shield noun The protective field produced by a Holtzman generator gear
Sietch (see-t-ch) place Fremen “place of assembly in times of danger.” Because the Fremen lived so long in peril, the term came by general usage to designate any cave warren inhabited by one of their tribal communities Fremen; desert
Stillsuit noun Body-enclosing garment invented on Arrakis. its fabric is a micro-sandwich performing functions of heat dissipation and filter of bodily waste. Reclaimed moisture is made available by tube from catchpockets Arrakis; Fremen
Thumper noun A short stake with spring-driven clapper at one end with the purpose of being driven into the sand and set to thumping to summon sandworms sandworms
Usul (oo-sool) name Fremen term translating to “the base of the pillar” Fremen; Paul Atreides
